### 5. Legal Terms / Conditions
*   **Premium:** Rupees 9,71,23,201/- (Nine Crore Seventy-One Lakh Twenty-Three Thousand Two Hundred One only).
*   **Annual Ground Rent:** Rupees 8,09,360/- (Rupees Eight Lakh Nine Thousand Three Hundred Sixty), payable annually between April 1st and June 30th. The rent is variable during the lease period as per the Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code (Determination and Re-determination of Land Revenue) Rules, 2018.
*   **Purpose:** The land shall be used for constructing a university, its maintenance, and ancillary purposes only.
*   **Taxes:** The Lessee is responsible for all rates, taxes, charges, and assessments on the land or buildings.
*   **Period of Construction:** Construction must commence within one year and be completed within three years from the lease commencement date.
*   **Assignment/Transfer:** The Lessee shall not assign, convey, sub-lease, or otherwise transfer the land or any rights without prior written consent of the Lessor. Any transfer must be reported to the Lessor within thirty days.
*   **Conditions for Charitable Institution:**
    *   The lease is non-transferable and cannot be partitioned.
    *   The Collector or authorized officers have the right to inspect the Lessee's accounting records.
    *   There shall be no discrimination based on caste, community, or religion in the institution's activities.
    *   Commercial use is permissible up to 10 percent of the constructed area.
*   **Re-entry:** The Lessor may re-enter and repossess the land if rent is in arrears for one calendar month or if the Lessee breaches any condition. The Lessor may also opt to recover a compounding amount instead of exercising the right of re-entry.
*   **Renewal:** The lease may be renewed for successive periods of 99 years at the Lessee's request and expense. Land rent and conditions may be revised upon renewal, with the Lessor's decision being final.
*   **Uninterrupted Use:** The Lessee is entitled to peaceful enjoyment of the land, provided rent is paid and conditions are met.

### 6. Summary
This is a Permanent Lease of Nazul Land agreement dated January 25, 2021, between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh (Lessor) and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development (Lessee). The agreement leases 20.234 hectares of land in Village Kanasaiya, Bhopal, for the purpose of constructing a university. The lease term runs until March 31, 2120. The Lessee has paid a premium of Rupees 9,71,23,201/- and an initial annual ground rent of Rupees 8,09,360/-. The agreement outlines strict conditions regarding land use, construction timelines, non-transferability, and the Lessor's right of re-entry for breach of contract, with provisions for renewal for 99-year periods.
