**6. Summary**
This document is a permanent Lease Deed registered on 27/01/2021, wherein the Governor of Madhya Pradesh (Lessor) leases 20.234 hectares of Nazul land in Village Kanasaiya, Bhopal (Khasra No. 40 & 51) to the Azim Premji Foundation for Development (Lessee). The lease is for the purpose of constructing a university and is valid until March 31, 2120. The Lessee has paid a premium of Rs. 97,123,201 and is liable for an annual ground rent of Rs. 8,09,360. The deed outlines specific conditions regarding construction timelines, non-transferability, permissible commercial use, and the Lessor's right of re-entry, with provisions for renewal for 99-year terms.
