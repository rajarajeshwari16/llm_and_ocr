### 5. Legal Terms / Conditions
*   **Premium:** Rupees 9,71,23,201/- paid via challan number 0029125053.
*   **Annual Land Rent:** Rupees 8,09,360/-, payable annually between April 1st and June 30th. The rent is variable during the lease period subject to the Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code (Determination and Re-determination of Land Revenue) Rules, 2018.
*   **Purpose:** The land shall be used exclusively for the construction and maintenance of a university and its ancillary purposes.
*   **Taxes:** The Lessee is responsible for all rates, taxes, charges, and assessments on the land or buildings.
*   **Construction Period:** Construction must commence within one year and be completed within three years from the lease commencement date.
*   **Assignment/Sub-lease:** The Lessee shall not assign, convey, sub-lease, or transfer the land or any rights without prior written consent of the Lessor.
*   **Conditions for Charitable Institution:**
    *   The lease is non-transferable and shall not be partitioned.
    *   The Collector or authorized officer has the right to inspect the Lessee's accounting records.
    *   There shall be no discrimination based on caste, community, or religion.
    *   Commercial use is permissible up to 10 percent of the constructed area.
*   **Re-entry:** The Lessor has the right of re-entry if rent is in arrears for one calendar month or if the Lessee breaches any condition. Alternatively, the Lessor may impose a compounding amount.
*   **Renewal:** The lease may be renewed for successive periods of 99 years at the Lessee's request and expense. Land rent and conditions may be revised upon renewal, with the Lessor's decision being final.
*   **Interpretation:** The terms "Lessor" and "Lessee" include their successors, assigns, heirs, executors, administrators, and representatives.

### 6. Summary
This Permanent Lease of Nazul Land, executed on January 25, 2021, is between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh (Lessor) and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development (Lessee). The Lessor grants a lease for 20.234 hectares of land in village Kanasaiya, District Bhopal, for a period ending March 31, 2120. The Lessee has paid a premium of Rupees 9,71,23,201/- and the first year's annual land rent of Rupees 8,09,360/-. The land is to be used exclusively for constructing a university, with construction to be completed within three years. The lease is non-transferable without the Lessor's consent and includes conditions regarding tax payments, maintenance, and the right of re-entry for the Lessor in case of breach. The lease is renewable for 99-year periods.
