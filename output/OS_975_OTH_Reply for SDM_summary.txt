**6. Summary**
A plot owner has filed a complaint against Azim Premchand University, making the following allegations:
*   The University committed "unauthorized encroachment" by breaking the complainant's fencing and display board, destroying the entrance path to their plot, and constructing an 8-inch cut concrete wall, 12-15 feet high, which has deprived them of access.
*   The University tampered with a government drain at the "triangular end" of the complainant's plot, changing its form and direction to create a path.
*   The complainant claims to be in "lawful possession" and was not given any information or opportunity for a hearing.
*   The complainant seeks the reopening of the path, payment for "damages/costs," and the removal of the alleged "illegal construction."

The University provides a point-wise response, stating:
*   It denies any "unauthorized encroachment" on private land.
*   All construction is being done on a plot provided by the government as per rules and after official measurement ("demarcation").
*   The University has obtained all necessary government permissions for the construction from Town and Country Planning, Madhya Pradesh Road Development Corporation, Kanhasaiya Panchayat, and the Forest Department.
*   It denies tampering with any drain, stating it constructed a water drainage line on its own government-provided land, which does not affect any privately owned land.
*   The University considers the complaint's points "irrelevant" because all construction is being done according to government rules and with permissions.
