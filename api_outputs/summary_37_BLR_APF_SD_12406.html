Summary

This Absolute Sale Deed was executed on December 18, 2002, by Mr. B.K. Jagannath, represented by his Power of Attorney holder, in favor of M/s Azim Premji Foundation Private Limited for a total sale consideration of Rs. 25,59,150. M/s Mainland Estates and Properties joined the transaction as a Confirming Party following a prior sale agreement dated November 30, 2001. The property involves 1 Acre 07 Guntas of land in Survey No. 69(P) at Doddakannelli Village, which was officially converted from agricultural to non-agricultural use in April 2002. The document provides an extensive title history dating back to 1937, tracing ownership through various sales, successions, and a family partition in 1979 that granted the Vendor absolute rights. The Vendor guarantees that the property is free from all liens, charges, and encumbrances and has handed over physical possession along with original title documents to the Purchaser. The Purchaser has paid the full consideration amount in various installments as acknowledged by the Vendor.
