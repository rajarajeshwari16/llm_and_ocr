Summary

This document is a Form B-I Kishtbandi Khatoni, which serves as a Record of Rights for the assessment year 2020-2021. The land is situated in Village Gadumariya, Tehsil Pussore, District Raigarh, and is held under Khata Number 91. The registered owner is the Azim Premji Education Trust, with Sunil Kumar Sah listed as the associated party from Bangalore. The property consists of five survey numbers totaling 1.3960 hectares of agricultural land. The record notes two significant mutation orders: one from August 2010 and another from May 2020, which updated the ownership or land status. The total land revenue and applicable cess for the property is recorded as 3.32. The document was digitally signed and certified as a true copy on June 2, 2021.
