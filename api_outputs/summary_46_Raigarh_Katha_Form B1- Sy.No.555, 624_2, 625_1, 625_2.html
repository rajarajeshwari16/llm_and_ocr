This document is a Form B-I Kishtbandi Khatoni (Asamivar) for the agricultural year 2020-2021, serving as a Record of Rights (ROR) for land located in Village Gadumariya, Tehsil Pusaur, District Raigarh. The land is jointly held by the Azim Premji Education Trust and Sunil Kumar Sahu, categorized under the General caste. The total land area recorded is 1.3960 hectares, which is distributed across five distinct survey numbers including 555, 624/2/ka, 625/1/ka/1, 625/1/ka/6, and 625/2. The document specifies a total land revenue demand of 3.32 rupees for the assessment period. Significant legal updates are noted through mutation orders dated 25-08-2010 and 20-05-2020. Finally, the record was digitally signed by Anand Sagar Bisi on 02-06-2021, certifying it as a true copy of the digitally published ROR.
