Summary:
This document is a Lease Deed executed on January 25, 2021, and registered on January 27, 2021, between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh (Lessor) and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development (Lessee). It pertains to the lease of 20.234 hectares of Nazul Land, identified by Khasra Nos. 40 and 51, located in Gram Kanasaiya, Tehsil Huzur, District Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. The lease is granted for a term commencing from the agreement date until March 31, 2120, primarily for the purpose of constructing and maintaining a university, with a provision allowing up to 10% of the built-up area for commercial use. The Azim Premji Foundation for Development paid a premium of Rupees 9,71,23,201/- and an annual ground rent of Rupees 8,09,360/-, in addition to a total stamp duty of Rupees 53,86,292/- and a registration fee of Rupees 36,72,472/-. The deed stipulates various conditions, including deadlines for construction commencement and completion, adherence to building regulations, and restrictions on transferring or sub-leasing the property without the Lessor's prior written consent. It also includes clauses for re-entry by the Lessor in case of non-payment of rent or breach of conditions, and provisions for the renewal of the lease for subsequent 99-year periods, subject to revised ground rent and conditions.
