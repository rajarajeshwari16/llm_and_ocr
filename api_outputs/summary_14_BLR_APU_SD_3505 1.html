Summary

This Absolute Sale Deed was executed on 12-10-2011 between Smt. K.P. Dayanandini and the Azim Premji Trust for the transfer of 3 Acres 26 Guntas of land in Sy.No. 65 of Boorgunte Village. The property was originally subject to land reform proceedings involving Rama Reddy and his heirs, eventually resulting in confirmed occupancy rights by the High Court of Karnataka in 1999. The Vendor acquired the property via a registered sale deed in 2005 and subsequently obtained an official conversion for industrial use in September 2011. The total sale consideration is fixed at Rs. 2,71,92,500/-, which the Vendor acknowledges receiving in full. The Vendor has delivered physical possession of the property and all original title documents to the Purchaser. Furthermore, the Vendor guarantees that the property is free from all liens, charges, and government acquisitions, promising to indemnify the Purchaser against any future title defects.
