Document Summary

Document Type

Notice Letter

Parties Involved

Complainant: Pritam Prasad Rawat, son of the late Shri Phoolchand Rawat ji. Respondent: The Manager, Institution Azim Premchand University. Issuing Authority: Court of Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal.

Property Details

A plot of land owned by Pritam Prasad Rawat, allegedly encroached upon by Azim Premchand University. The university is located in Village Kanasaiya, Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal. The complainant resides at A-74 Indra Vihar Colony, Airport Road, Bhopal.

Key Dates

Document Date: 26.03.2025. Required Court Appearance Date: 28/03/2025.

Legal Terms

Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil, Notice Letter, unauthorized encroachment, ex-parte proceedings.

Summary

This document, identified as Serial No./975/Reader/2025, is a formal notice letter issued by the Court of the Sub-Divisional Magistrate in Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal, on March 26, 2025. It is directed to the Manager of Azim Premchand University regarding a complaint filed by Pritam Prasad Rawat. The complaint alleges that the university has committed unauthorized encroachment on a plot of land owned by Mr. Rawat. The notice legally summons the university's manager to appear before the court on March 28, 2025, to present their side of the matter. The document carries a significant legal implication, warning that if the university representative fails to appear, the court will initiate ex-parte proceedings, meaning a judgment may be passed in their absence based solely on the complainant's claims.
