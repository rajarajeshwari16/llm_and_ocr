Summary:
This document is a point-wise response from a university to a complaint filed by a plot owner. The complainant alleges that the university has committed unauthorized encroachment by destroying their plot's fencing, display board, and access path, and has constructed a 12-15 feet high concrete wall, thereby denying them access. The complainant also accuses the university of illegally altering the course of a government drain. The university refutes all claims, stating that its construction activities are confined to the land officially allotted to it by the government after proper measurement. It asserts that it has obtained all necessary permissions from authorities including the Town and Country Planning, Madhya Pradesh Road Development Corporation, Kanhasaiya Panchayat, and the Forest Department. The university denies encroaching on any private land and clarifies that it has only constructed a drainage line on its own property without affecting any other land. Consequently, the university deems the complainant's requests for removal of the construction and restoration of access as baseless ('nirarthak').
