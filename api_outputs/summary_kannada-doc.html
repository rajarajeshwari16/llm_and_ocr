Summary

This document is a Mutation Copy (M.R. Number H4) for the transaction year 2015-2016, pertaining to land located in Andhralu Village, Ballari Taluk. The mutation was initiated following a court order from the Assistant Commissioner's Office (Order No. Kam.Aa/417/2014-15) dated March 15, 2015, specifically for a data correction and RTC change. The property involved is Survey Number 133/*/4, with a total area of 4.00 acres, out of which 3.45 acres are held by M/s Azim Premji Educational Trust. The Trust is represented by its Power of Attorney holder, B A Vijaya Kumar, under Khata Number 159. This legal action updates the Record of Rights, Tenancy and Crops (RTC) to reflect the current possession and rights as established by a prior partition dated March 19, 2013. The final mutation order was officially accepted and signed by the Revenue Inspector of Ballari Hobli on July 31, 2015, ensuring the official land records align with the judicial directives and administrative corrections.
