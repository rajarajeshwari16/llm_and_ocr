Summary

This document is a Mutation Copy from the year 2015-2016, recording a change in land records for a property in Andralu village, Ballari district. The change was mandated by a court order from the Hon'ble Sub-Divisional Officer's Office, Ballari, under Order No. Kam/Pahani/Kam A/417/2/2014-2015, dated 15-03-2015. The property affected is Survey Number 133/*/4, where M/s Azim Premji Educational Holder - B V Vijaya Kumar is the possessor of 3.45 acres out of a total of 4.00 acres under Khata Number 159. This mutation, identified as M.R. Number H4, represents a 'Pahani Change' and supersedes a previous entry related to a partition from 19/03/2013 (MR H25/2012-2013). The mutation was officially approved and finalized by the Revenue Inspector of Hobli Ballari on 31/07/2015, legally updating the land possession records as per the court's directive.
