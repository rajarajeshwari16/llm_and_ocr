Document Summary

Document Type

Formal Response to a Legal Notice

Parties Involved

Sender: Naveen Das, Registrar, Azim Premji University, Bhopal; Recipient: Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal; Complainant: Pritam Prasad Rawat.

Property Details

The dispute concerns an alleged unauthorized encroachment by Azim Premji University on a plot of land owned by Pritam Prasad Rawat. Supporting documents mentioned include Khasra, Aksh, and a Lease Deed.

Key Dates

Document Date: 17/04/2025; Information Letter Date: 26/03/2025 (Reference No. 975/Reader/2025); In-person Appearance Date: 28th March 2025; Deadline for Response: 17th April 2025.

Legal Terms

Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil Huzur, unauthorized encroachment, Hon'ble Court, Development permission, Service road, Occupancy certificate, Demarcation order, Khasra, Aksh, Lease Deed, Registrar.

Summary

This document is a formal response from Azim Premji University, Bhopal, to the Sub-Divisional Magistrate of Tehsil Huzur, regarding a complaint of unauthorized land encroachment filed by Pritam Prasad Rawat. The university was notified via a letter dated March 26, 2025, and after an initial appearance on March 28, 2025, was granted until April 17, 2025, to present its case. In this letter, the university's Registrar, Naveen Das, clarifies a misnomer in the complaint (Azim Premchand University instead of Azim Premji University) and submits a detailed, point-wise rebuttal to the allegations. To substantiate its position and refute the encroachment claim, the university has enclosed a comprehensive set of legal and administrative documents, including development permissions, road approvals, an occupancy certificate, a demarcation order, Khasra, Aksh, and the Lease Deed. The submission is intended to provide the court with factual information to resolve the dispute.
