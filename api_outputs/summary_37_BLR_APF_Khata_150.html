Summary

This document is a Khatha Certificate and a Property Register Extract (Form 1) issued by the Bruhat Bangalore Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) on June 30, 2015. It officially certifies that the property located in Doddakannelli Village, Marathahalli Division, stands in the name of M/s Azim Premji Foundation Trust in the municipal records. The property is identified by multiple numbers including 590/69/71/2 & 3, 71/4, 133, and 134, with a total measurement area of 16-16 A-Gu 7, 14, 384 Sqft. The certificate includes a legal proviso stating that the Khatha is subject to cancellation under Section 114A of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act 1976 if any disputes or objections are raised. A processing fee of Rs. 125.00 was paid via Challan No. Jun/0711 at Bangalore. The document was processed by D. Vijayakumari and signed by the Assistant Revenue Officer of the Gottigere/Marathahalli sub-division.
