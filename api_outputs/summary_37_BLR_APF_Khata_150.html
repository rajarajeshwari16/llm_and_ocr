Summary

This document is a Khata Certificate and Extract issued by the Bruhat Bangalore Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) for the assessment year 2015-2016. It officially records that the property situated in Doddakannahalli village, Ward 150, stands in the name of M/s. Ajivani Premanjali Foundation Trust (Azim Premji Foundation). The property is identified by several khata numbers, specifically 530/530/69/71/243, 71/4, 133, and 134, with a total area measurement of 16-16 A-Gu and 384 Sqft. The certificate was signed and issued on June 30, 2015, by the Assistant Revenue Officer of the Marathahalli Sub-Division. A crucial legal condition mentioned is that the khata is liable for cancellation under Section 114 or 114A of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976, should any property dispute arise. Additionally, the document notes a challan payment of Rs. 125.00 for the processing of this record.
