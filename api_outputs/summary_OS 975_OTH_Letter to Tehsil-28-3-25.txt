Document Summary
================

Document Type:
Official Correspondence / Response to Information Letter

Parties Involved:
Sender: Naveen Das (Registrar, Azim Premji University); Authorized Representatives: Chandrashekhar and Mohan Singh; Recipient: Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal; Complainant: Pritam Prasad Rawat.

Property Details:
A plot of land in Bhopal allotted to Azim Premji University by the government, on which construction work is being carried out. A complaint of unauthorized encroachment has been made by Pritam Prasad Rawat regarding his own plot of land.

Key Dates:
Document Date: 28/03/2025; Reference Information Letter Date: 26/03/2025.

Legal Terms:
Sub-Divisional Magistrate, Tehsil Huzur, Information letter, unauthorized encroachment, allotted, measurement, government permissions, authorized to be present in person, Registrar.

Summary:
This document is a formal response dated 28/03/2025 from Naveen Das, the Registrar of Azim Premji University, to the Sub-Divisional Magistrate of Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal. It is written in reference to an information letter (No. 975/Reader/2025) from the Magistrate's office dated 26/03/2025. The letter addresses a complaint filed by an individual named Pritam Prasad Rawat, who alleges that the university has committed unauthorized encroachment on his land. The university refutes this claim, stating that its construction activities are confined to the plot of land that was legally allotted to it by the government, following proper measurement and after securing all necessary government permissions. In compliance with the Magistrate's directive, the university is authorizing two representatives, Chandrashekhar and Mohan Singh, to appear in person to present the university's case. Legally, this document serves as the university's official statement of defense and confirms its cooperation with the inquiry initiated by the complaint.
