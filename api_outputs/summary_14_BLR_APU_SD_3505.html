Document Summary

Document Type

Absolute Sale Deed

Parties Involved

Vendor: Smt. K.P. Dayanandini, D/o K.M. Prabhakara Reddy (also a member of M/s Daya Associates); Purchaser: Azim Premji Trust, a Registered Private Trust acting through its Trustee Azim Premji Trustee Company Private Limited, represented by authorized representative Mr. V.R. Ganesh.

Property Details

Landed property bearing Survey No. 65, measuring 3 Acres 26 guntas, situated at Boorgunte Village, Sarjapura Hobli, Anekal Taluk, Bangalore Urban District. The property is converted from agricultural to industrial use (Order No. ALN(A)(SH)SR:50/11-12). Boundaries: East by Survey Nos. 67 and 68, West by Road, North by Survey No. 66, and South by Survey No. 64.

Key Dates

Execution Date: 12-10-2011; Registration Date: 19-10-2011; Conversion Order Date: 08-09-2011; Vendor's Acquisition Date: 18-07-2005; Land Tribunal Order: 25-05-1979 and 21-10-1998.

Legal Terms

Absolute Sale, Occupancy Rights, Karnataka Land Reforms Act, Karnataka Stamp Act 1957, Land Conversion (Agricultural to Industrial), Indemnity, Encumbrance-free, Khata, General Power of Attorney, Gift Deed.

Summary

This Absolute Sale Deed, executed on October 12, 2011, and registered on October 19, 2011, details the sale of 3 Acres 26 guntas of land in Survey No. 65 of Boorgunte Village from Smt. K.P. Dayanandini to the Azim Premji Trust for a total consideration of Rs. 2,71,92,500. The document traces the property's title history back to Sri Rama Reddy, who was granted occupancy rights under the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, and whose heirs later sold the land to the Vendor in 2005. Prior to this sale, the Vendor obtained a formal conversion of the land from agricultural to industrial use through the Deputy Commissioner of Bangalore District. Legally, the Vendor warrants that the property is free from all liens, charges, and encumbrances, and provides a specific indemnity clause to protect the Purchaser against any future title defects. The Purchaser paid a total stamp duty of Rs. 18,49,110 and registration fees of Rs. 2,71,925 to finalize the transaction. This deed effectively transfers absolute ownership, vacant physical possession, and all associated easementary rights to the Azim Premji Trust for industrial development purposes.
