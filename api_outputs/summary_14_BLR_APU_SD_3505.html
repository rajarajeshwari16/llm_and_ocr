Summary

This document is an Absolute Sale Deed executed on October 12, 2011, in Sarjapura, Bangalore. It records the sale of a property by the Vendor, Smt.K.P.DAYANANDINI, to the Purchaser, AZIM PREMJI TRUST, represented by Mr.V.R.GANESH. The property in question is 3 Acres and 26 guntas of land in Sy.No.65, located in Boorgunte Village, Anekal Taluk, which has been converted from agricultural to industrial use. The document traces the property's title, originating from a grant of occupancy rights to the heirs of Sri.Rama Reddy by the Land Tribunal, which was confirmed by the High Court of Karnataka. The Vendor acquired the property from these heirs on July 18, 2005. For a total sale consideration of Rs.2,71,92,500/-, which the Vendor acknowledges as fully received, she transfers all her rights, title, and interest in the property to the Purchaser absolutely and forever. The Vendor has delivered vacant possession and assures the property is free from all encumbrances, litigation, and claims.
