Summary

This document is an Absolute Sale Deed executed on October 12, 2011, in Bangalore, formalizing the sale of a parcel of land. The Vendor, Smt.K.P.DAYANANDINI, sold the property to the Purchaser, PREMJI AZIM TRUST, represented by Mr.V.R.GANESH, for a total consideration of Rs.2,71,92,500/-. The property in question is land bearing Sy.No.65, measuring 3 Acres 26 Guntas, located in Boorgunte Village, Sarjapura Hobli, Anekal Taluk, Bangalore Urban District, which has been duly converted from agricultural to non-agricultural (industrial) purposes by an official order dated September 8, 2011. The deed meticulously details the property's complex ownership history, including initial occupancy rights granted to Sri.Rama Reddy by the Land Tribunal, subsequent legal challenges and remands, and eventual confirmation of occupancy rights to his legal heirs, from whom the current Vendor ultimately derived her title. The Vendor assures the Purchaser of clear and marketable title, vacant physical possession, freedom from encumbrances, and indemnifies the Purchaser against any future defects in title, while the Purchaser agrees to cooperate in transferring revenue records and Khata. This deed signifies the complete transfer of all rights, title, and interest in the specified land, now designated for industrial use, from the Vendor to the Purchaser, with all associated legal formalities and financial transactions completed.
