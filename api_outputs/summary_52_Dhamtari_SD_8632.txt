Summary:
This Sale Deed, executed on March 13, 2012, records the transfer of agricultural land in Village Shankardah, Dhamtari, from Shri Banshilal Sahu and Shri Manoharlal Sahu to the Azim Premji Educational Trust. The total sale consideration is Rs. 46,37,500 for a property measuring 1.06 hectares under Khasra No. 376/1. The sellers inherited the land from their late father, Parasram Sahu, and confirmed that all previous encumbrances, including a mortgage with Central Bank of India, have been cleared. The transaction involved an advance payment in November 2011 and final payments via HDFC Bank checks in March 2012. The document confirms the transfer of absolute ownership, physical possession, and all associated easement rights to the buyer. The property is declared free from all legal disputes, government acquisitions, and ceiling limit violations under the Chhattisgarh Agricultural Land Ceiling Act 1960.
