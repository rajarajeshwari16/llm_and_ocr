Summary

This Official Memorandum, issued by the Special Deputy Commissioner of Bangalore District on 03-11-2008, grants permission for land conversion to Smt. K.P. Dayanandini for industrial purposes. The conversion pertains to a total of 3-16 Acres/Guntas across Survey Nos. 49 and 72/2 located in Burugunte Village, Anekal Taluk. The applicant paid a total fee of Rs. 5,92,526, which includes land conversion and Mody fees, following the approval of the Single Window Committee. The order is subject to several conditions, including obtaining necessary approvals from the Bangalore Development Authority or B.M.R.D.A. and adhering to pollution control norms. Furthermore, the land must be utilized for the specified industrial purpose within two years, and any violation of the conditions may lead to the cancellation of the order and penalties under the Karnataka Land Revenue Act. The document also specifies boundary details and requires the Tahsildar to update the RTC and land revenue records accordingly.
