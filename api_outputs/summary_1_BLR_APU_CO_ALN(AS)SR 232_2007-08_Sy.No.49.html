Summary

This document is an Official Memorandum issued by the Special Deputy Commissioner of Bangalore District, granting permission for land conversion from agricultural to non-agricultural industrial use. The applicant, Smt. K.P. Dayanandini, sought conversion for a total of 3 acres and 16 guntas of land located in Burugunte Village, Sarjapura Hobli, Anekal Taluk, comprising Survey Numbers 49 and 72/2. The order follows the payment of a total conversion fee of Rs. 5,92,526/- and adheres to the provisions of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act 1964. Several conditions are imposed, including the requirement to obtain approvals from BDA, BMRDA, or the Pollution Control Board before commencing industrial activities. The applicant is mandated to utilize the land for the specified industrial purpose within two years and must maintain specific road margins as per government regulations. Furthermore, the order stipulates that any violation of these conditions will result in the cancellation of the conversion and potential demolition of unauthorized structures without compensation.
