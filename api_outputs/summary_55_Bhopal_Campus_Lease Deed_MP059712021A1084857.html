Summary

This document is a Permanent Lease Deed of Nazul Land executed on January 25, 2021, between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development. The lease grants 20.234 hectares of land in Village Kanasaiya, Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal, specifically for the construction and maintenance of a university. The foundation paid a premium of Rs. 9,71,23,201 and is liable for an annual ground rent of Rs. 8,09,360, which is subject to periodic revision. The lease term is set to expire on March 31, 2120, with a provision for 99-year renewals. Key conditions include a requirement to commence construction within one year and complete it within three years, and a strict prohibition on transferring or sub-leasing the land without prior government approval. The property is strategically located on the Bhopal Bypass Road and is designated for educational use.
