Summary

This document is a formal Lease Deed executed between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development for the allotment of Nazul land. The lease pertains to a total area of 20.234 hectares situated in Gram Kanasaiya, Tehsil Huzur, District Bhopal, specifically covering parts of Khasra numbers 40 and 51. The lessee paid a premium of Rs. 9,71,23,201 and an initial annual ground rent of Rs. 8,09,360 for the purpose of constructing and maintaining a university. The lease term is set to expire on March 31, 2120, with provisions for successive renewals of 99 years each. Legally, the document stipulates that the land must be used exclusively for educational and ancillary purposes, with construction required to commence within one year and complete within three years. Furthermore, the deed includes strict clauses regarding non-transferability without prior consent and the lessor's right of re-entry in the event of rent defaults or breaches of conditions.
