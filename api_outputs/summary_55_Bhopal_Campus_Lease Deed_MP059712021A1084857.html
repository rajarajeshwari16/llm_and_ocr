Summary

This document is a Permanent Lease Deed for Nazul Land executed on January 25, 2021, between the Governor of Madhya Pradesh as the Lessor and the Azim Premji Foundation for Development as the Lessee. The lease pertains to a total area of 20.234 hectares situated in Village Kanasaiya, Tehsil Huzur, District Bhopal, specifically comprising portions of Khasra numbers 40 and 51. The land is granted for the specific purpose of constructing and maintaining a university, with a lease term extending until March 31, 2120, and a provision for successive 99-year renewals. The Lessee paid a premium of Rs. 9,71,23,201 and an initial annual ground rent of Rs. 8,09,360, following a recommendation by the Madhya Pradesh Cabinet for the charitable organization. Legally, the deed imposes strict conditions including a requirement to complete construction within three years, a prohibition on transferring or partitioning the land without prior permission, and a right of re-entry for the Lessor in case of rent default or breach of conditions. The property is strategically located on the Bhopal bypass road and is classified for educational use, with the deed ensuring that the foundation's activities remain non-discriminatory.
