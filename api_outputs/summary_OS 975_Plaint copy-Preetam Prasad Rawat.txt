Summary:
This document is a formal complaint filed by Pritam Prasad Rawat with the Sub-Divisional Officer of Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal. Mr. Rawat alleges that his neighbor, Azim Premchand University, has illegally encroached upon his property, which is Plot No. 01 of Khasra No. 63/1 in Village Kanhe Saiya. He claims that on October 8, 2022, he discovered that the university had broken his plot's fencing and display board, constructed a 12-15 feet high concrete wall, and thereby blocked his access to the land. The complaint further states that the university tampered with a government drain to create a path. Mr. Rawat asserts his lawful possession and title, stating that these actions were taken without any prior notice or hearing. He requests the authorities to take appropriate action to remove the illegal construction, reopen the blocked path, and grant him damages and expenses, attaching a copy of the sale deed as proof of ownership.
