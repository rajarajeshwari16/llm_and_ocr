Summary:
Pritam Prasad Rawat has filed a complaint with the Sub-Divisional Officer of Tehsil Huzur, Bhopal, regarding his property in Village Kanhe Saiya. He states that he is the owner in possession of Plot No. 01, part of Khasra No. 63/1, measuring 1168.43 sq.ft. Upon inspection on 08-10-2022, he discovered that the neighboring Azim Premchand University had committed unauthorized encroachment. The university allegedly broke his plot's wire fencing and display board, destroyed the access path, and illegally constructed an 8-inch thick, 12-15 feet high concrete wall, thereby preventing him from accessing his property. The complainant also alleges that the university has tampered with a government drain. He claims that despite previously informing university representatives (Vijit Mukherjee, Surendra Reddy, and Chandrashekhar) of his ownership, he was given no notice or opportunity for a hearing before the construction. The complainant requests appropriate action against the responsible parties, removal of the illegal construction, restoration of access to his plot, and compensation for damages and costs.
