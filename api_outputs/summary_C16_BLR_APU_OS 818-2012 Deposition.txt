Summary:
This document records a witness statement given on 16-07-2022 in Case No. 818/2012 before the Fourth Additional Civil Judge and Pr.D.A., Magistrate Court, Anekal. The plaintiff, identified as P.W. 1, was administered an oath and underwent chief examination by their advocate, Sri P.A., affirming the truthfulness of an affidavit submitted as evidence. During this examination, the plaintiff formally submitted and marked eight certified copies of documents as exhibits, including sale deeds dated 08-08-2011 (Ex.P.1), 11-05-2011 (Ex.P.2), 25-07-2005 (Ex.P.4), 05-06-1967 (Ex.P.7), and 02-11-1972 (Ex.P.8), a Muktiyar sale deed dated 30-06-2005 (Ex.P.3), and land conversion orders dated 04-06-2011 (Ex.P.5) and 07-03-2011 (Ex.P.6). Significantly, the cross-examination of the plaintiff was recorded as "nil" because the defendant's advocate was absent, no statement of objection was filed, and defendant numbers 2 and 3 were declared ex-parte. This indicates that the plaintiff's testimony and the extensive documentary evidence presented went unchallenged at this stage of the court proceedings, potentially strengthening the plaintiff's position regarding the property transactions involved in the case.
