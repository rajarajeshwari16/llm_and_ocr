Summary

This Sale Deed records the transfer of agricultural land in Village Shankardah, Dhamtari, from Shri Banshilal Sahu and Shri Manohar Lal Sahu to the Azim Premji Educational Trust. The property, identified as Khasra No. 376/1 with an area of 1.06 hectares, was sold for a total consideration of Rs. 46,37,500. The sellers acquired the property through inheritance from their late father, Parasram Sahu, and declared it free from all encumbrances, mortgages, and legal disputes. An advance of Rs. 3,00,000 was paid in November 2011, and the remaining balance of Rs. 43,37,500 was settled via HDFC Bank checks in March 2012. The document confirms that physical possession of the unirrigated Dhanha land has been handed over to the purchaser. The deed was formally executed and registered in March 2012 at the Sub-Registrar's office in Dhamtari, Chhattisgarh, with all stamp duties and registration fees paid.
